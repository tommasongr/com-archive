Novel Hairline

Novel Sans is a humanist grotesque typeface released by Atlas Font Foundry. The new hairline styles were realized in order to extend the family with a set of 24 ultra light weights. To find more informations, please check the website atlasfonts.com.
Project realized as production assistant at Bureau Christoph Dunst – Berlin – 2013 / 2014
