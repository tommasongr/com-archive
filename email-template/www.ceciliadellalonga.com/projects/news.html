ITALIANS' BEHAVIOURS
ON THE INTERNET

_
OnePage Website. Interactive article on Italians' skills on the Internet. In a world where the digital system is becoming the main vehicle, the reading experience is completely changed and reinvented. Links and interactivity change the relationship between readers and information. But who are (and who are not), the italian techno-readers? Among web-addicted and web-reluctant only 44% say to read news online. Young people do not seem to be interested and elders are not very digitalized. This website offers an overview of the Italian behaviors in this area of interest, identifying four main (and extreme) user profiles.
