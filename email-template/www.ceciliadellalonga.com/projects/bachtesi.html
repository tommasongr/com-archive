{ EDITORIAL DESIGN PROJECT }
{ WORKING TIME: march to july 2013 }
{ PROJECT FOR: Politecnico di Milano }

BACHELOR THESIS

_
A book about a social advertising campaign against racism. The aim is to present the entire campaign focusing on the creative process, analysing every detail step by step. The purpose is to reconstruct the whole project showing the peculiarities and explaining the choises made during the project's phase. The social campaign is a three phases useful project studied to help immigrants to integrate themselves in Milano. I've worked on the first part designing the advertising campaign.

[ To read the book or to take a look of the whole project go here > ]

Tell our stories and our experiences, for some reason, is something we normally do and we used to ask for some story back. We usually trust friends’ opinions as well as known people’s viewpoint. We have confidence in someone who’s open to give advice, in a friend’s face, in a positive witness. Many of us need a hand to be involved and accompanied into a new journey. Right in this situation the project find an important place: the need to find in the social dimension an help to the individual life. Work on this project has been a challenge and a good reason to study and deepen new topics. A challenge due to the delicate issue and a lesson to the thematic context and the social design in general. From the cultural and design point of view the project has been an enrichment value.
